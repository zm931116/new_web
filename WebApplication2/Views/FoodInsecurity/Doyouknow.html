The reality is that 3.6 million Australians (15%) have experienced food insecurity at least once in the last 12 months.

Almost half of food insecure Australians (48%) are employed in some way, whether full-time, part-time or casually.

Our youngest members of the community are also impacted, as dependent children live in 40% of food insecure households

Those effected from food insecurity come from all age groups, backgrounds, and circumstances.
